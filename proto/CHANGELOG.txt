## [0.7.0] - 2026-05-16

### Added
- `quantum.pulse.v1/feedback.proto`: FeedbackConfig and FeedbackResult
  messages for the Lyapunov feedback controller introduced in QubitOS
  v0.7.0. FeedbackConfig carries the controller type (only "lyapunov"
  is implemented in v0.7.0), per-axis feedback gains, optional length-9
  full 3x3 cross-axis gain matrix (opt-in surface, not validated),
  feedback delay in nanoseconds, maximum correction amplitude, active
  control axes, and the target density matrix used by the Lyapunov
  function. FeedbackResult embeds an SMEResult for the controlled run
  alongside the per-step correction history, Lyapunov function
  trajectory, cumulative feedback energy cost, crossover noise
  strength (populated by the noise-sweep analysis only), and the
  DecoherenceBudget consumed per qubit by feedback-delay cycles.

## [0.6.0] - 2026-05-16

### Added
- `quantum.pulse.v1/sme.proto`: SMEConfig and SMEResult messages for the
  stochastic master equation solver introduced in QubitOS v0.6.0.
  SMEConfig carries measurement efficiency, collapse-operator specs,
  measurement operator override, RNG seed, and per-trajectory storage
  flags. SMEResult carries the conditional density matrix, trajectory
  fidelity / purity series (when stored), the measurement record, and
  diagnostics for max trace deviation and positivity violations.

## [0.5.0] - 2026-02-09

### Added
- `quantum.pulse.v1/temporal.proto`: TimePoint, AWGClockConfig, TemporalConstraint, PulseSequence, DecoherenceBudget messages
- `quantum.error.v1/error_budget.proto`: ErrorBudget tracking and analysis messages

## [0.1.0] - 2026-02-07

Initial release of QubitOS protocol definitions.
