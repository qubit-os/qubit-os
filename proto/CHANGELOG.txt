## [Unreleased]

## [0.6.0] - 2026-05-16

### Added
- `quantum.pulse.v1/sme.proto`: SMEConfig and SMEResult messages for the
  stochastic master equation solver introduced in QubitOS v0.6.0.
  SMEConfig carries measurement efficiency, collapse-operator specs,
  measurement operator override, RNG seed, and per-trajectory storage
  flags. SMEResult carries the conditional density matrix, trajectory
  fidelity / purity series (when stored), the measurement record, and
  diagnostics for max trace deviation and positivity violations.

## [0.5.0] - 2026-02-09

### Added
- `quantum.pulse.v1/temporal.proto`: TimePoint, AWGClockConfig, TemporalConstraint, PulseSequence, DecoherenceBudget messages
- `quantum.error.v1/error_budget.proto`: ErrorBudget tracking and analysis messages

## [0.1.0] - 2026-02-07

Initial release of QubitOS protocol definitions.
